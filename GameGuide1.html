Introduction

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena game. In League of Legends, players assume the role of a summoner that controls a champion, with unique abilities and battle against a team of other players or computer-controlled champions. The goal is usually to destroy the opposing team's Nexus, a structure that lies at the heart of a base protected by defensive structures.

How To Play

To move your champion, right click on the terrain where you'd like to go To attack an enemy, right click on your target To cast a spell left-click the associated icon or use the spell's hotkey (Q,W,E, or R by default), and then click on the target.

Game Basics

Lanes: Roads that connect your base to your enemies
Minions: Soldiers controlled by AI that spawn at the nexus and move toward the enemyâ€™s base, fighting enemies they encounter on the way. You can earn coins for killing minions.
Turrets: Defensive structures that defend each lane at even intervals
Inhibitors: Located where the lanes meet the base. A super minion will spawn with each new wave of minions if the inhibitor is destroyed.
Nexus: The heart of the base that periodically spawn minion waves in each lane. Destroying the nexus of the opposing player will win the game.
Levels:You earn XP while playing the game which allow you to level up and unlock game modes, summoning spells and loot.
